The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The vision of future Bangladesh is one of a prosperous, sustainable, and inclusive nation, characterized by economic growth, technological advancement, and social equity. Aspiring to achieve upper-middle-income status by 2031 and high-income status by 2041, Bangladesh seeks to diversify its economy, enhance industrial and digital sectors, and embrace sustainable practices to combat climate change. The future envisions a modernized infrastructure with smart cities, renewable energy, and efficient transportation networks, alongside universal access to quality education, healthcare, and digital connectivity. With a strong focus on empowering its youth and fostering innovation, Bangladesh aims to harness its demographic dividend and establish itself as a global hub for trade and technology. By promoting good governance, reducing inequality, and strengthening global partnerships, Bangladesh seeks to create a resilient and thriving society that ensures a higher quality of life for all its citizens while preserving its rich cultural heritage.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
